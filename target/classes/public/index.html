PLAY


Reversi is a 1 or 2-player strategy board game played on an 8Ã—8 uncheckered board. There are sixty-four identical game pieces called discs that have a light and dark side. Players take turns placing their color discs on the board until they are out of moves.During a play, your reverse your opponent's discs by placing your disc next to one of theirs. You change opponent disc colors by placing your discs adjacent to one of their discs and one of your own. If both these conditions are met, you change the color of all opponent discs in a straight line from the placed one. The player with the most discs in their color, once both players are out of moves, wins.
